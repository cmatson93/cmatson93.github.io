Get to Know Me

My name is Christina. I grew up in Kansas City Missouri and am currently living in the Bay Area. My passions include, sports, travelling, eating good food and coding of course! After graduating from college with a degree in Biology I moved to Hong Kong and taught English. While doing that I began to teach myself Python. I found the challenges coding brought when trying to solve problems and the excitement that followed the solving of those problems to be rewarding and enjoyable.

A few months after moving to the Bay Area I came across the Web Development Program at UC Berkeley and knew it was something I was interested in. After completing the course I have now gained practical skills that I can use to create technology to make our world a better place and would love to do that by working for you and your company!
